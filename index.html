I’m Lucas Cucco, originally from Rio de Janeiro and currently based in London, where I work in Master Data Management within the retail industry.

Outside work, I enjoy watching sports, especially football. I support Flamengo in Brazil, and I also like playing football, tennis and going for runs from time to time.
